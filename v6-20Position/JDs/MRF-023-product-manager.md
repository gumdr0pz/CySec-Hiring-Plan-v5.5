## 1. Role Purpose

The Product Manager owns the product roadmap and requirements for the BU's offerings, turning market needs, client feedback, and the cyber roadmap into a prioritised, deliverable plan. The role exists to align engineering, cybersecurity, and sales around a coherent product direction, manage the backlog and release cadence, and ensure the BU ships the right capabilities at the right time. As a Wave 1 near-term core hire on the CTO org chart, the Product Manager operationalises the product-management layer that underpins predictable delivery and scalable growth. This is a general role with a dotted-line responsibility to the Cybersecurity Practice.

## 2. Key Responsibilities

- **Own the product roadmap and requirements** for the BU's offerings, balancing client needs, commercial goals, and the cybersecurity roadmap into a clear, prioritised plan.
- **Manage the backlog and releases**, defining epics, user stories, and acceptance criteria, and grooming and prioritising the backlog for delivery teams.
- **Coordinate across engineering, cyber, and sales**, aligning stakeholders on scope, sequencing, and trade-offs, and serving as the single point of accountability for product direction.
- **Work with the Cybersecurity Practice on a dotted line**, ensuring product decisions reflect cyber requirements, compliance obligations, and sensitive-work compartmentalisation.
- **Define and track product KPIs and outcomes**, using adoption, satisfaction, and revenue signals to steer the roadmap.
- **Plan and run release cycles**, coordinating launch readiness, documentation, and go-to-market alignment with Sales and Presales.
- **Translate technical capability into client value**, partnering with the Technical Presales and Sales on positioning, demos, and proposals.
- **Lead discovery and validation**, running customer interviews, market analysis, and competitive reviews to de-risk investment decisions.

## 3. Required Qualifications & Experience

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Business, or a related field (or equivalent experience).
- 6+ years of experience in product management, technical program/product ownership, or a comparable role, with demonstrated ownership of a roadmap end to end.
- Proven experience coordinating cross-functional engineering, security, and commercial teams.
- Strong backlog management, prioritisation, and release-planning skills within Agile delivery.
- Excellent stakeholder management and communication with both technical and executive audiences.

## 4. Preferred / Desirable Qualifications

- Experience with cybersecurity, blockchain, or B2B technology products.
- Familiarity with Malaysian / regional market and regulated-industry requirements.
- MBA or relevant postgraduate qualification.
- Experience defining and operating product metrics and analytics.

## 5. Technical Skills & Competencies

- Roadmap definition, prioritisation frameworks (e.g., RICE, MoSCoW), and outcome-based planning.
- Backlog and release management with Agile / Scrum (epics, user stories, acceptance criteria, sprint and release planning).
- Requirements management and collaboration tooling (e.g., Jira, Confluence, Azure DevOps).
- Product discovery, validation, and competitive / market analysis.
- Working awareness of cybersecurity domains and delivery contexts sufficient to coordinate with the Cybersecurity Practice.
- Data-informed decision-making using product analytics and KPIs.

## 6. Behavioural Competencies

- **Ownership** — takes end-to-end accountability for the roadmap and product outcomes.
- **Strategic thinking** — balances short-term delivery with long-term product direction and trade-offs.
- **Communication & influence** — aligns diverse stakeholders without direct authority.
- **Collaboration** — partners effectively across engineering, cyber, and sales.
- **Customer focus** — grounds decisions in real client and market needs.
- **Integrity & discretion** — handles sensitive cyber and client information responsibly.

## 7. Certifications

**Required**

- None mandatory.

**Preferred**

- Product management certifications (e.g., Pragmatic Institute, SAFe POPM).
- Agile / Scrum certification (e.g., CSPO, PSPO).

## 8. Key Performance Indicators

- A maintained, prioritised product roadmap aligned with engineering, cyber, and sales within the first 6 months.
- Predictable release cadence with backlog readiness and on-time delivery of committed scope.
- Measurable product outcomes (adoption, satisfaction, revenue contribution) against agreed KPIs.
- Effective cross-functional alignment, evidenced by reduced scope churn and rework.
- Successful collaboration with the Cybersecurity Practice on cyber-aligned product decisions.
- Positive stakeholder feedback from engineering, cyber, sales, and BU leadership.

## 9. Reporting & Team Context

This is a general role reporting on a dotted line to the BU Head, with a dotted line to the Head of Cybersecurity Practice for cyber-aligned product decisions. As a Level 5 Manager-grade role, the Product Manager leads the Product / PM team — including the Business Analyst — and coordinates closely with engineering, cybersecurity, sales, and presales across the BU.
