## BUDGET / CAP NOTE

At the Rate-card benchmark, annual burdened cost (RM3,257,280 – RM4,427,136) is **27.1% – 36.9%** of RM12M revenue, **above the old 25% cap (RM3.0M)**. Per the v6 decision the cap is **relaxed** — either raise it to ~**37%** of revenue, or grow revenue to ~**RM17.7M** to fit 25%. The 36-month burdened runway is **RM8,216,640 – RM11,117,952**. See `CySec-Financial-Analysis-v6.md`.
