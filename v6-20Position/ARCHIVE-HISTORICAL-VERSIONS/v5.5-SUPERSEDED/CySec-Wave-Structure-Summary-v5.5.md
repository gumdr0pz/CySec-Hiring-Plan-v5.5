## Design Principle: Supportive at Every Stage

Each wave stands up functional capability that can deliver, pairing seniors with junior capacity where it scales output:

- **Wave 1 (7):** PreSales and DevSecOps as senior + junior pods, a Technical Account Manager for post-sale delivery, plus governance and GRC seniors.
- **Wave 2 (5):** Revenue closers (Enterprise + Government Sales) and senior technical practice leadership (OffSec Lead + Red Team, CTI Lead) — triggered by signed SOWs.
- **Wave 3 (8):** The scale-up bench — junior/IC support across OffSec, CTI, VORON, and PM, plus the Blockchain senior.

> **Structure notes:** The Senior Project Manager and Junior Professional Services roles have been **removed** from the structure. Professional Services is a single Technical Account Manager; the PM Team is a Business Analyst + Junior PM (Wave 3) reporting on a dotted line to the BU Head.
