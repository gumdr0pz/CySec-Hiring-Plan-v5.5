## WAVE 1 OVERVIEW

### 1.1 Purpose

This document details the immediate hiring requirements for the Cybersecurity Business Unit (BU). Wave 1 establishes the foundational infrastructure and platform capabilities required to support subsequent hiring waves and business operations.

### 1.2 Key Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Positions to Hire** | 5 |
| **Timeline** | Months 1-3 |
| **Trigger** | Board approval of BU establishment |
| **Annual Cost (Min)** | RM423,360 |
| **Annual Cost (Max)** | RM677,376 |
| **Monthly Cost (Min)** | RM35,280 |
| **Monthly Cost (Max)** | RM56,448 |

### 1.3 Strategic Rationale

Wave 1 establishes the foundational capabilities required for:

1. **Security Governance**: Compliance and risk management framework
2. **Platform Security**: Security automation and CI/CD security
3. **Consulting Capability**: Immediate client engagement
4. **Project Management**: Delivery coordination
5. **Service Delivery**: Professional services capability

#### 3.1.3 Responsibilities

1. **Security Governance**
   - Lead government security compliance and governance initiatives
   - Develop and maintain security policies and procedures
   - Coordinate with regulatory bodies on compliance requirements

2. **Project Management**
   - Manage government security projects and delivery timelines

**Desirable:**
- Experience with government procurement processes
- Knowledge of critical infrastructure protection

- PMP or PRINCE2 certification

## HIRING TIMELINE

### 4.1 90-Day Execution Plan

```
Week 1-2: Preparation
├── Finalize job descriptions
├── Set up hiring workflows
├── Identify interview panels
└── Launch employer branding

Week 3-4: Sourcing
├── Post on job boards (LinkedIn, JobStreet, Indeed)
├── Activate employee referral program
├── Engage recruitment agencies
└── Review applications

Week 5-6: Screening
├── Phone screens
├── Technical assessments
├── Initial interviews
└── Shortlist candidates

Week 7-8: Interviews
├── Technical interviews
├── Cultural fit interviews
├── Management interviews
└── Final assessments

Week 9-10: Selection
├── Reference checks
├── Offer letters
├── Negotiations
└── Background checks

Week 11-12: Onboarding
├── Pre-boarding preparation
├── Day 1 orientation
├── Team introductions
└── 30-60-90 day plans
```
