## 1. Role Purpose

The Junior Professional Services Consultant supports the delivery of the Cybersecurity BU's professional and consulting engagements, helping clients implement and adopt security and blockchain solutions. Working alongside senior consultants, this role ensures that implementation tasks, documentation, and client support are executed accurately and on time. It is an entry point into a consulting career, providing hands-on exposure to real client engagements while strengthening the BU's reputation for reliable, high-quality service delivery.

## 2. Key Responsibilities

- Support senior consultants throughout the engagement lifecycle, contributing to planning, configuration, and delivery of client solutions.
- Assist with hands-on implementation tasks, ensuring deployments are completed accurately and aligned with the agreed project scope.
- Document implementation procedures, configurations, and runbooks to create clear, reusable references for clients and internal teams.
- Facilitate client training sessions, helping users understand and adopt delivered solutions effectively.
- Resolve client issues by triaging requests, troubleshooting basic problems, and escalating complex matters to senior consultants.
- Maintain accurate project records and status updates to keep stakeholders informed of progress and outstanding items.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams across the Cybersecurity BU to ensure smooth, coordinated service delivery.
- Develop technical and consulting knowledge continuously to take on increasing responsibility within engagements.

## 3. Required Qualifications & Experience

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
- At least 1 year of experience in technical support or consulting.
- Basic technical knowledge of cybersecurity or blockchain concepts.
- Strong written and verbal communication skills.
- Demonstrated problem-solving aptitude and a willingness to learn.

## 4. Preferred / Desirable Qualifications

- Exposure to client implementation projects.
- Familiarity with service delivery methodologies (e.g., ITIL or structured project frameworks).
- Technical writing skills for documentation and procedures.
- Prior customer service or client-facing experience.

## 5. Technical Skills & Competencies

- Foundational understanding of cybersecurity and/or blockchain technologies.
- Ability to follow and document technical implementation steps and configurations.
- Basic troubleshooting and issue triage skills.
- Comfort with standard productivity, ticketing, and documentation tools.
- Awareness of service delivery and project management concepts.
- Capacity to learn new tools, platforms, and frameworks quickly.

## 6. Behavioural Competencies

- **Communication:** Conveys information clearly to both technical and non-technical clients.
- **Ownership:** Takes responsibility for assigned tasks and follows them through to completion.
- **Collaboration:** Works effectively with senior consultants and cross-functional teams.
- **Analytical Thinking:** Approaches problems methodically and seeks practical solutions.
- **Adaptability:** Adjusts quickly to changing client needs and project priorities.
- **Eagerness to Learn:** Actively seeks feedback and develops new skills.

## 7. Certifications

**Required**

- None mandatory.

**Preferred**

- Entry-level cybersecurity certification (e.g., CompTIA Security+).
- Foundational blockchain or cloud certification.
- ITIL Foundation or equivalent service management credential.

## 8. Key Performance Indicators

- Successfully supports assigned implementation tasks with minimal rework within the first 6 months.
- Produces clear, accurate implementation documentation accepted by senior consultants and clients.
- Contributes to client training sessions with positive participant feedback.
- Resolves or appropriately escalates client issues within agreed response times.
- Achieves consistently positive feedback from senior consultants on reliability and quality of work.
- Demonstrates measurable growth in technical and consulting capability over the first 12 months.

## 9. Reporting & Team Context

This role reports to the Senior Professional Services Consultant and works closely with the broader Professional Services team and cross-functional specialists within the Cybersecurity BU on client engagements.

## 10. Compensation & Benefits

- **Monthly Base Salary:** RM3,500 – RM5,600
- **Annual Base Salary:** RM42,000 – RM67,200

Benefits include statutory EPF, SOCSO, and EIS contributions, medical coverage, training and certification sponsorship, and a performance-based bonus.

## 11. Career Progression

The natural next step for this role is progression to Professional Services Consultant, taking on greater engagement ownership and client-facing responsibility before advancing toward senior consulting roles.
