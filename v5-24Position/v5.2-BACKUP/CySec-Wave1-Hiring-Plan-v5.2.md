## WAVE 1 OVERVIEW

### 1.1 Purpose

This document details the immediate hiring requirements for the Cybersecurity Business Unit (BU). Wave 1 establishes the foundational infrastructure, platform capabilities, and revenue-generating teams required to support subsequent hiring waves and business operations.

### 1.2 Key Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Positions to Hire** | 8 |
| **Timeline** | Months 1-3 |
| **Trigger** | Board approval of BU establishment |
| **Annual Cost (Min)** | RM677,376 |
| **Annual Cost (Max)** | RM1,083,840 |
| **Monthly Cost (Min)** | RM56,448 |
| **Monthly Cost (Max)** | RM90,320 |

### 1.3 Strategic Rationale

Wave 1 establishes the foundational capabilities required for:

1. **Security Governance**: Compliance and risk management framework
2. **Platform Security**: Security automation and CI/CD security
3. **Consulting Capability**: Immediate client engagement
4. **Project Management**: Delivery coordination
5. **Service Delivery**: Professional services capability
6. **Revenue Generation**: Sales and presales team to drive pipeline
7. **Business Development**: Enterprise and government sales coverage
