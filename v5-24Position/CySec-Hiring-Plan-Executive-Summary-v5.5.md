## EXECUTIVE OVERVIEW

This executive summary presents the strategic hiring plan for the Cybersecurity Business Unit (BU). The plan details the recruitment of **20 positions** across **9 teams** over a 12-month period, structured in three revenue-triggered waves that build deliverable capability at each stage:

- **Wave 1 (7)** stands up PreSales and DevSecOps as senior+junior pods, a Technical Account Manager for delivery, plus governance/GRC seniors.
- **Wave 2 (5)** adds revenue closers (Enterprise + Government Sales) and senior technical practice leadership (OffSec + CTI).
- **Wave 3 (8)** scales the Junior/IC bench and brings the Blockchain senior online.

The former **Senior Project Manager** and **Junior Professional Services** roles have been removed; the PM Team is a Business Analyst + Junior PM reporting to the BU Head, and Professional Services is a single Technical Account Manager.

## STRATEGIC ALIGNMENT

### Supportive at Every Stage

```
Wave 1 (Immediate)  → PreSales + DevSecOps pods + TAM + governance/GRC
Wave 2 (RM4M+ SOWs) → Sales closers + OffSec/CTI senior practice leadership
Wave 3 (RM8M+ Rec.) → Junior/IC scale-up bench + Blockchain senior
```

**Why this works:** Pairing each Wave 1 senior with junior capacity where it scales (PreSales, DevSecOps) lets those functions deliver from day one. Senior practice leads (OffSec, CTI) and sales closers arrive in Wave 2 once SOWs justify them; the Junior/IC bench fills out in Wave 3 on recurring revenue.
