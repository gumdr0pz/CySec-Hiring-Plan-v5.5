## STRATEGIC ALIGNMENT

### Platform-First Approach (Option C)

The hiring plan follows a **Platform-First** strategy, prioritizing infrastructure and platform capabilities before scaling delivery teams:

```
Wave 1 (Immediate) → Infrastructure & Platform
Wave 2 (RM4M+) → Delivery & Business Development
Wave 3 (RM8M+) → Scale & Optimization
```

### Revenue-Triggered Scaling

Hiring waves are tied to confirmed revenue milestones, ensuring financial sustainability:

| Wave | Trigger | Positions | Annual Cost | % of Total |
|------|---------|-----------|-------------|------------|
| **Wave 1** | Board Approval | 7 | RM593K – RM948K | 34.3% |
| **Wave 2** | RM4M+ SOWs | 7 | RM593K – RM948K | 34.3% |
| **Wave 3** | RM8M+ Recurring | 7 | RM544K – RM871K | 31.4% |

**Note:** v5.3 uses balanced 7-7-7 structure. Previous versions had 5-7-9 or 8-4-9 splits (deprecated).

## RISK ASSESSMENT

### High Priority Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Talent shortage | Medium | High | Competitive compensation, employer branding |
| Revenue shortfall | Medium | High | Flexible wave triggers, cost optimization |
| Extended hiring timelines | High | Medium | Parallel hiring tracks, talent pipeline |

### Mitigation Strategies

1. **Talent Acquisition**
   - Competitive compensation packages
   - Strong employer branding
   - Employee referral programs
   - University partnerships

2. **Financial Management**
   - Revenue-triggered hiring
   - Regular budget reviews
   - Cost optimization initiatives
   - Flexible wave timing

3. **Operational Excellence**
   - Structured onboarding programs
   - Mentorship initiatives
   - Knowledge management systems
   - Cross-training programs
