## 1. Role Purpose

The Senior Project Manager exists to ensure the Cybersecurity BU delivers its offensive security, threat intelligence, DevSecOps, blockchain security, and consulting engagements on time, within scope, and on budget. The role provides the delivery backbone that turns signed statements of work into predictable client outcomes, coordinating specialist teams and managing risk so that technical experts can focus on the work. By standardising how projects are planned and governed, this role directly protects margin, client trust, and the BU's reputation for reliable execution.

## 2. Key Responsibilities

- Lead end-to-end delivery of a portfolio of cybersecurity projects, owning timelines, milestones, scope, and budget across concurrent client engagements.
- Coordinate cross-team dependencies between the offensive security, threat intelligence, DevSecOps, blockchain security, and consulting practices to keep delivery seamless and unblocked.
- Develop, document, and continuously refine project management methodologies, templates, and governance standards that scale with the growing BU.
- Communicate clear, timely project status, forecasts, and decisions to internal leadership and external client stakeholders, tailoring detail to each audience.
- Drive proactive risk and issue management, maintaining risk registers, escalating early, and implementing mitigation plans before delivery is impacted.
- Manage project financials, tracking budgets, resource utilisation, and burn against signed SOWs to protect profitability and flag variances early.
- Facilitate Agile/Scrum ceremonies and delivery cadences, removing impediments and keeping multidisciplinary teams aligned to sprint and milestone goals.
- Build and maintain strong client relationships throughout delivery, ensuring expectations are managed and engagements convert into repeat and recurring business.

## 3. Required Qualifications & Experience

- Bachelor's degree in Business, Computer Science, or a related field.
- 5+ years of experience in project management, including ownership of full delivery lifecycles.
- PMP, PRINCE2, or an equivalent recognised project management certification.
- Hands-on experience applying Agile/Scrum methodologies in a delivery environment.
- Strong stakeholder management skills across both technical teams and client-facing audiences.

## 4. Preferred / Desirable Qualifications

- Master's degree in Business Administration (MBA).
- Experience delivering cybersecurity or technology projects.
- Working knowledge of security frameworks (e.g., ISO 27001, NIST, OWASP).
- Demonstrated budget and financial management experience on client engagements.

## 5. Technical Skills & Competencies

- Project planning, scheduling, and tracking tools (e.g., Jira, MS Project, Confluence, Trello, or equivalent).
- Agile/Scrum and hybrid delivery frameworks, including sprint planning, backlog management, and ceremonies.
- Risk management techniques: risk registers, RAID logs, issue escalation, and mitigation planning.
- Budget, resource, and utilisation tracking against signed SOWs.
- Stakeholder reporting, status dashboards, and executive-level communication artefacts.
- Familiarity with cybersecurity delivery contexts (penetration testing, threat intelligence, DevSecOps, blockchain security) is an advantage.

## 6. Behavioural Competencies

- Ownership and accountability: takes full responsibility for delivery outcomes and follows through to completion.
- Communication: conveys complex status and risk clearly and confidently to both technical and executive audiences.
- Collaboration: builds trust across specialist teams and brokers alignment on shared dependencies.
- Analytical thinking: anticipates risks, interprets delivery data, and makes sound, evidence-based decisions.
- Composure under pressure: maintains clarity and prioritisation when managing concurrent, high-stakes engagements.
- Continuous improvement: actively strengthens delivery practices and shares lessons learned.

## 7. Certifications

**Required**
- PMP, PRINCE2, or an equivalent recognised project management certification.

**Preferred**
- Certified ScrumMaster (CSM), PMI-ACP, or equivalent Agile certification.
- Security-related certifications or training (e.g., ISO 27001 Foundation) are a plus.

## 8. Key Performance Indicators

- On-time, on-budget delivery for at least 90% of managed projects within the first 6–12 months.
- A standardised project management methodology and governance toolkit adopted across the PM Team.
- Accurate, on-cadence status reporting to stakeholders with zero material unreported risks.
- Effective risk and issue management, evidenced by early escalation and timely mitigation of critical items.
- Positive client satisfaction scores and demonstrable contribution to repeat or expanded engagements.
- Healthy budget variance and resource utilisation across the managed portfolio.

## 9. Reporting & Team Context

This role reports to the BU Head on a dotted-line basis and sits within the PM Team, working closely with the offensive security, threat intelligence, DevSecOps, blockchain security, and consulting practices as well as external client stakeholders. As a senior delivery lead, the role is expected to set delivery standards and mentor junior project staff as the team scales.
