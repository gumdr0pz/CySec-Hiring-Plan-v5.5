## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### 1.1 Overview

This document outlines the strategic hiring plan for the Cybersecurity Business Unit (BU): **20 positions** across **9 teams** over a 12-month period, structured in three revenue-triggered waves that build deliverable capability at each stage — pairing seniors with junior capacity where it scales.

### 1.2 Key Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Total Positions** | 23 |
| **Existing Leadership** | 3 |
| **Positions to Hire** | 20 |
| **Hiring Timeline** | 12 months |
| **Revenue Target** | RM12,000,000 |
| **Wave Structure** | 7-5-8 |
| **Rate Bands** | 10 Senior/Lead + 10 Junior/IC |
| **Total Annual Cost (Min)** | RM1,451,520 |
| **Total Annual Cost (Max)** | RM2,322,480 |
| **Cost as % of Revenue** | 12.1% – 19.4% |
| **25% Revenue Cap** | ✅ Within |
| **Headroom** | RM677,520 – RM1,548,480 |

### 1.3 Strategic Alignment

1. **Supportive at Every Stage**: Wave 1 hires PreSales and DevSecOps as senior+junior pods so they deliver immediately.
2. **PreSales-First Capability**: Wave 1 anchors on PreSales (Sr + Jr) plus platform and governance seniors to win early pipeline.
3. **COGS Optimization**: Labor costs below 25% of revenue via a 30% salary reduction.
4. **Revenue-Triggered Scaling**: Tie Waves 2 and 3 to confirmed revenue (RM4M+ SOWs, RM8M+ recurring).

### 1.4 Wave Structure Summary (7-5-8)

| Wave | Trigger | Positions | Level Mix | Annual Cost Range | % of Total |
|------|---------|-----------|-----------|-------------------|------------|
| **Wave 1** | Board Approval | 7 | 4 Senior + 3 Junior | RM520,128 – RM832,224 | 35.8% |
| **Wave 2** | RM4M+ SOWs | 5 | 3 Senior + 2 Lead | RM423,360 – RM677,400 | 29.2% |
| **Wave 3** | RM8M+ Recurring | 8 | 1 Senior + 7 Junior/IC | RM508,032 – RM812,856 | 35.0% |
| **Total** | - | **20** | - | **RM1,451,520 – RM2,322,480** | **100%** |

**Wave 1 Positions (7):** Presales Consultant ⭐, Jr Presales, Gov Sec PM, Sr GRC Specialist, Sr DevSecOps, Jr DevSecOps, Technical Account Manager

> **Note:** The former Senior Project Manager and Junior Professional Services roles have been removed from the structure. The PM Team is a Business Analyst + Junior PM reporting on a dotted line to the BU Head; Professional Services is a single Technical Account Manager.
