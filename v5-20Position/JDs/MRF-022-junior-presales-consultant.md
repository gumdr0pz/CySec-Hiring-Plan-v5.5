## 1. Role Purpose

The Junior Presales Consultant supports the Cybersecurity BU's presales motion, working directly under the Senior Presales Consultant to build demos, proofs-of-concept, and technical collateral that move opportunities through the pipeline. As an early Wave 1 hire, this role provides the hands-on capacity that lets the senior presales engine scale — preparing environments, drafting proposal sections, and handling technical research so the BU can respond to more opportunities, faster.

## 2. Key Responsibilities

- Assist the Senior Presales Consultant in scoping client requirements and preparing technical proposals and solution outlines.
- Build and maintain demo environments and proof-of-concept (POC) setups for cybersecurity and blockchain offerings.
- Draft sections of RFP/RFI responses and assemble supporting technical documentation.
- Research competitor offerings, products, and frameworks to strengthen technical positioning.
- Maintain and organise the presales collateral library (demo scripts, datasheets, reference architectures).
- Coordinate with delivery and consulting teams to confirm proposed scopes are feasible.
- Capture lessons from won/lost deals to improve reusable presales assets.

## 3. Required Qualifications & Experience

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Information Technology, or a related field (or equivalent experience).
- 1–2 years in a technical, presales-support, or solution-support role (internships count).
- Foundational understanding of cybersecurity and/or blockchain concepts.
- Strong written communication and willingness to learn solution engineering.

## 4. Preferred / Desirable Qualifications

- Exposure to demo/POC environment setup or lab building.
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP).
- Interest in pursuing a presales / solution-engineering career path.

## 5. Technical Skills & Competencies

- Basic solution documentation and diagramming.
- Demo/POC environment configuration support.
- Technical research and competitive analysis.
- Comfort with productivity, presentation, and collaboration tooling.

## 6. Behavioural Competencies

- Eagerness to learn and grow into a solution-engineering role.
- Clear communication and attention to detail.
- Collaborative across sales, delivery, and consulting.
- Ownership of assigned collateral and POC tasks.

## 7. Certifications

**Required**

- None mandatory.

**Preferred**

- Foundational cybersecurity or cloud credentials (e.g., Security+, cloud fundamentals).

## 8. Key Performance Indicators

- Turnaround time and quality of demo/POC environments prepared.
- Volume and reuse of presales collateral maintained.
- Contribution to RFP/RFI responses delivered on schedule.
- Senior Presales Consultant satisfaction with support provided.
- Demonstrated growth in solution-engineering knowledge within 6–12 months.

## 9. Reporting & Team Context

This role reports to the Senior Presales Consultant within the Sales team and works closely with sales, delivery, and consulting colleagues across the Cybersecurity BU. It is a junior individual-contributor role focused on learning and scaling presales capacity.

## 11. Career Progression

The natural next step is Presales Consultant / Solution Engineer, taking ownership of opportunities and solution design within the Cybersecurity BU.
