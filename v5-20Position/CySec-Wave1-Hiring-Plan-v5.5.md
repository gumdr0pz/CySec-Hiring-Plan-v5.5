## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Wave 1 is a 7-role foundation** — PreSales and DevSecOps as senior+junior pods, a Technical Account Manager for post-sale delivery, plus governance and GRC seniors. v5.5 uses a 7-5-8 wave structure (20 hires).

### Key Changes from v5.4

| Aspect | v5.4 (8-5-8) | v5.5 (7-5-8) |
|--------|--------------|--------------|
| Wave 1 | 8 (4 Sr + 4 Jr) | 7 (4 Sr + 3 Jr) |
| Removed | — | Junior Professional Services |
| Professional Services team | TAM + Jr PS | TAM only |
| Total hires | 21 | 20 |
| Total Cost | RM1,512,000 – 2,419,248 | **RM1,451,520 – 2,322,480** |

**Rationale:** Removing the Junior Professional Services role leaves the Technical Account Manager as the single Professional Services hire in Wave 1, trimming cost while retaining the PreSales and DevSecOps support pods.
