## 1. Role Purpose

The Senior Professional Services Consultant is the engagement leader who turns the Cybersecurity BU's offensive security, threat intelligence, DevSecOps, and blockchain security capabilities into successfully delivered client outcomes. The role exists to safeguard delivery quality and client satisfaction across consulting engagements while strengthening the frameworks and presales motion that drive repeatable, profitable growth. By owning client relationships end to end, the consultant directly underpins the BU's revenue milestones and reputation in the Malaysian and regional market.

## 2. Key Responsibilities

- Lead professional services engagements from kickoff to closeout, ensuring scope, timelines, budgets, and technical objectives are met across cybersecurity and blockchain projects.
- Own and manage client relationships and expectations, acting as the primary point of contact and trusted advisor throughout the engagement lifecycle.
- Ensure consistent service quality and high client satisfaction by setting delivery standards, conducting reviews, and resolving issues before they impact outcomes.
- Develop and refine service delivery frameworks, methodologies, and reusable assets that improve consistency, efficiency, and margin across the Professional Services team.
- Support presales activities by scoping engagements, contributing to proposals and Statements of Work, and providing technical credibility in client conversations.
- Coordinate cross-functional delivery teams spanning offensive security, threat intelligence, and DevSecOps to bring the right expertise to each engagement.
- Monitor engagement health and commercial performance, flagging risks, change requests, and expansion opportunities to BU leadership.
- Capture lessons learned and client feedback to continuously improve delivery practices and inform service offering development.

## 3. Required Qualifications & Experience

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
- Minimum 5 years of experience in professional services or consulting.
- Technical background in cybersecurity or blockchain.
- Strong client management skills, with a track record of managing expectations and building lasting relationships.
- Demonstrated experience applying structured service delivery methodologies.

## 4. Preferred / Desirable Qualifications

- Master's degree in a relevant field.
- Experience with enterprise solution implementation.
- Previous experience at a consulting firm.
- Exposure to business development or revenue-generating activities.

## 5. Technical Skills & Competencies

- Solid working knowledge of cybersecurity domains such as offensive security, threat intelligence, or DevSecOps, and/or blockchain security.
- Engagement and delivery management using methodologies such as Agile, Waterfall, or hybrid models.
- Proficiency with project and service delivery tooling (e.g., Jira, Confluence, MS Project, or equivalent).
- Ability to author and review Statements of Work, proposals, scoping documents, and delivery reports.
- Understanding of security frameworks and standards (e.g., ISO 27001, NIST, OWASP) sufficient to guide client engagements.
- Strong commercial awareness of utilisation, margin, and engagement profitability.

## 6. Behavioural Competencies

- Client-centric communication: translates technical complexity into clear value for both technical and executive stakeholders.
- Ownership and accountability: takes end-to-end responsibility for engagement outcomes and follows through under pressure.
- Collaboration: works effectively across specialist teams to assemble and lead high-performing delivery groups.
- Analytical thinking: diagnoses delivery and client issues early and drives pragmatic resolutions.
- Adaptability: navigates shifting client priorities and multiple concurrent engagements with composure.

## 7. Certifications

**Required**

- None mandatory.

**Preferred**

- Project or delivery certifications such as PMP, PRINCE2, or Agile/Scrum.
- Security certifications such as CISSP, CISM, OSCP, or relevant blockchain security credentials.

## 8. Key Performance Indicators

- Client satisfaction (CSAT) scores meeting or exceeding the BU target across managed engagements within the first 6-12 months.
- On-time, on-budget delivery for a majority of led engagements.
- Measurable improvement in delivery consistency through adopted frameworks and reusable assets.
- Personal billable utilisation maintained at target levels.
- Contribution to presales, evidenced by proposals or SOWs supported and converted.
- Reduction in escalations and rework across engagements under management.

## 9. Reporting & Team Context

This role reports on a dotted line to the BU Head and works closely with delivery specialists across offensive security, threat intelligence, DevSecOps, and blockchain security, as well as the presales and account teams. As a senior member of the Professional Services team, the consultant also guides and mentors junior delivery staff and sets the standard for engagement execution.

## 10. Compensation & Benefits

Base salary range: **RM4,900 – RM7,840 per month** (**RM58,800 – RM94,080 annually**). The package includes statutory EPF, SOCSO, and EIS contributions, medical coverage, training and certification sponsorship, and a performance-based bonus.

## 11. Career Progression

The natural next step is into a Lead or Principal Consultant role, or a Professional Services Manager position with broader responsibility for the practice, its people, and its commercial performance.
