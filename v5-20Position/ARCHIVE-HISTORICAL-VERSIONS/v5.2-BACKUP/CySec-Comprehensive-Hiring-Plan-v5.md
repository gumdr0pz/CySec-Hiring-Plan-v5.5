### 1.1 Overview

This document outlines the strategic hiring plan for the Cybersecurity Business Unit (BU) of [Company Name]. The plan details the recruitment of **21 positions** across **9 teams** over a 12-month period, structured in three waves aligned with revenue milestones.

The hiring plan supports three core strategic objectives:

3. **Revenue-Triggered Scaling**: Tie hiring waves to confirmed revenue milestones to ensure financial sustainability
